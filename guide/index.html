Introduction

Homewerksww.com is built upon the Drupal Content Management system. Drupal allows for easy management of nearly all sections of the site.


Logging In

In order to edit any portion of the site, the user must be logged in.

  1. Navigate your browser to  http://www.joycone.com/user/login
  2. Enter your  Username and Password

Drupal Basics

From the Drupal user login, all portions of the site can be accessed by top menu that appears. This portion of the CMS is referred to as the Admin Menu

The most important areas of the system are as follows:

  1. Content management > Content
  2. Content management > Create content
  3. -- Homewerks -- > Special Pages

This guide will walk through how to edit each. As you will see, much of Drupal is laid out in a similar manner and uses the same adding, deleting, editing, saving, etc. conventions.

Which is What?

You are now ready to edit Joy Cone.com, but how do you know what part of the site is what? The guide details both the Admin Pages and the front end website they relate to, by showing images of both.


Navigation Menus

The Main Navigation menu on Homewerksww.com is on the left under the Joy Cone logo.

SKUs

Many of the elements and content of Joy Cone.com (About, Terms, etc.) are Drupal content management items. As far as editing goes, PRODUCT/SKUs are the easiest to edit in a Drupal CMS, as they are presented in an easy-to-use WYSIWYG(What You See Is What You Get.)

Adding a SKU

To add a SKU:

  1. Creating a SKU is the first required step before making a Product.
  2. From the Drupal Admin Menu, click the Content management > Create content > SKU list item
  3. A content creation section will appear:
  4. The most important fields are SKU, DESCRIPTION, PART NO and the QUANTITY.
    1. To add a SKU, enter the SKU in the text box.
    2. Add a Description of the SKU to the text field below the SKU text box.
    3. Add SKU again after the description in the input text labeled PART NO.
    4. Include the Quantity of the product in the text box.
  5. When a PRODUCT is finished being added, be sure to save.

Editing a SKU

To edit a SKU:

  • From the website homepage click Products on the left sidebar and select the appropriate category for your product
  • You can enter the SKU into the search bar or enter a product keyword to locate the product to edit.
  • A list of categories will appear, click on the corresponding Category for the product you wish to edit. Most categories have sub-categories.
  • When you're logged on and in the correct sub-category for your product you'll see Edit this product under the Product Name and Edit at the bottom of the product container below Part No,Description and Carton Qty.
  • You can edit a SKU by clicking the Edit text below the Part No,Description and Carton Qty.
  • The link will take you to an Edit page for that SKU.
  • Edit the SKU in the top text box.
  • Edit the DESCRIPTION of the SKU in the textfield.
  • Edit the PART NO. in the text box by entering the SKU.
  • Edit the QUANTITY for the SKU.
  • After making edits for the SKU make sure to SAVE at the bottom of the page
  • Review all edits to the products by clicking the Preview button to ensure structure after saving.

Products

The Joy Cone site uses Drupal for generating products. Drupal is a very powerful system that allows you to manage products. In this guide, we will cover the most common and important tasks you will be using while updating the Joy Cone website

Adding a Product

To add a Product:

  1. Before starting make sure you've created a SKU for the corresponding product.
  2. From the Drupal Admin Menu, click the Content management > Create content > Product list item
  3. A content creation section will appear:
  4. The important fields are TITLE, IMAGE, BODY, PRODUCT DETAILS PDF, SKUs and the VOCABULARIES.
  5. To add a TITLE, enter the Product name in the text box labeled TITLE.
  6. Add a Description of the product in the textfield labeled BODY located below the IMAGE field.
  7. To add a product PDF, click Choose File under the Product Details PDFs section and select your PDF to upload. You will want to make sure you click Upload.
  8. Add SKU, that you created in step 1, after the description in the input text labeled SKUs.
  9. Drupal appends [nid:] after the SKU, do not delete it.
  10. The right sidebar labeled VOCABULARIES let's you add CATEGORIES, BRANDS and PUCKS.
  11. To select multiple CATEGORIES hold CTRL while clicking on each category.
  12. BRANDS should only be changed when a product is at the end of it's lifespan or if it does need to be updated.
  13. To select multiple PUCKS hold CTRL while clicking on each puck.
  14. Unselecting a category/puck is the same process as selecting, hold CTRL and click the highlighted category/puck.
  15. When a Product is finished being added, be sure to save.

Editing a PRODUCT

To edit a PRODUCT:

  1. From the website homepage click PRODUCTS on the left sidebar and select the appropriate category for your product.
  2. You can enter the SKU into the search bar or enter a product keyword to locate the product to edit.
  3. A list of categories will appear, click on the corresponding Category for the product you wish to edit. Most categories have sub-categories.
  4. When you're logged on and in the correct sub-category for your product you'll see Edit this product under the Product Name.
  5. You can edit a Product by clicking the Edit this product text below the product name.
  6. The link will take you to an Edit page for that product.
  7. Edit the TITLE of the product in the text field on the top.
  8. Edit the IMAGE of the product by clicking Remove and uploading a new image.
  9. Edit the DESCRIPTION in the textfield labeled BODY.
  10. Edit the SKUs to the corresponding product in the text box labeled SKUs.
  11. After entering the edits for the product make sure to SAVE at the bottom of the page.
  12. The right sidebar labeled VOCABULARIES let's you edit CATEGORIES, BRANDS and PUCKS.
  13. To select multiple CATEGORIES hold CTRL while clicking on each category.
  14. BRANDS should only be changed when a product is at the end of it's lifespan.
  15. To select multiple PUCKS hold CTRL while clicking on each puck.
  16. Unselecting a category/puck is the same process as selecting, hold CTRL and click the highlighted category/puck.
  17. Review all edits to the website to ensure structure after saving.

Product Images

To add your main product image, you'll see the IMAGE field with a Choose File button. Once here, you can upload your image.

  • First, you will click Choose File and select the image you want to upload from your local machine.
  • You MUST select the Upload button next to Choose File and keep the file size under 8MB or it won't upload.
  • Save changes and your new image will appear as a thumbnail on the product page.

Product Details PDFs

To add a product PDF, click Choose File under the Product Details PDFs section and select your PDF to upload. You will want to make sure you click Upload.

Product SKUs

To add a SKU to a product, navigate to the SKUs field under the Product Details PDFs section and enter the corresponding SKU(s).

Drupal appends [nid:] after the SKU, do not delete it.

Product Categories

The first selection field in the right sidebar has the list of categories for the site.

You can select a category by clicking once or by holding CTRL and clicking each respective category.

Unselecting a category is the same process as selecting, hold CTRL and click the highlighted category.

Product Brands

To pick a BRAND use the dropdown menu under the PRODUCT CATEGORIES selection field, each product only has one BRAND.

When a product is near the end of it's lifespan you'll change it to Closeout.

Product Pucks

The third selection field in the right sidebar, under BRANDS, has the list of pucks that can be applied to a product.

You can select a puck by clicking once or by holding CTRL and clicking each respective puck.

Unselecting a puck is the same process as selecting, hold CTRL and click the highlighted puck.

Editing Product Categories

We've covered how to add Products to a Category but there are a couple other things we can do with a Category page that we haven't discussed

  1. Navigate to the Admin Menu on top and click Content Management > Taxonomy
  2. If you need to edit the categories currently in use you can click list terms.
  3. Next you'll need to select the edit button for the category you want to edit.
  4. The Edit page has 2 fields you will use.
  5. The TERM NAME is the category title.
  6. The DESCRIPTION is the category description.
  7. When you're done editing the category make sure to save.
  8. A few final steps to complete the process include navigating to SITE CONFIGURATION > TAXONOMY MENU.
  9. On this page you'll need to uncheck the Product Categories or Brands and save.
  10. After you save, you will need to recheck the box you unchecked to refresh the menu when you add a new category.
  11. Check the order of the menus in Taxonomy > List Terms to make sure your order is consistant.

Unpublishing Products

  • In order to remove a Product click on Content Management > Content
  • Three drop down menus will appear with published, Blog entry and Around the Home.
  • First you will need to select Product from the drop down menu
  • Next you will need to select a Product Category from the third drop down menu and press filter
  • Select the checkbox next to your product and select Unpublish from the drop down menu under Update options, don't DELETE products.
  • Finally press Update

Publishing Products

  • In order to publish a Product that's been unpublished click on Content Management > Content
  • First you will need to select not published from the drop down menu and filter the results
  • Finally press Update and your product(s) will publish

Add Users

  1. When logged in navigate to the User Management menu and select Users > Add user.
  2. A page for creating a user will pull up and make sure Add user tab is selected and highlighted in blue.
  3. The Add user page requires a list of information about the new user. The following is required:
  4. Username
  5. E-Mail Address
  6. Password
  7. Notify user of new account should be checked so they receive an email.
  8. After entering all the information click Create new account
  9. Your new user can log into the site at http://www.joycone.com

Change Password

  1. When logged in navigate to the User Management menu and select Users > Add user.
  2. You can change the password for the user by clicking on edit on the right side of the user under Operations.
  3. The new screen contains the Username, E-mail address and Password, to change to a new password enter both fields Password and Confirm password.
  4. Scroll to the button of the page and click save to confirm your new password.

Important Information

Use Save button to save the item you are editing so you don't lose your update.

Do not DELETE any item, simply Unpublish it instead.

For Product Description, in the WYSIWYG you will be able to format the copy according to conventional HTML tags. You should only be selecting from Paragraph and Heading (1,2,3,4,5,6) for your copy. A Paragraph is your standard text. A Heading is a title or sub title. If you are confused as to what formats to use, copy an paste from one product page you'd like to emulate to the new one you are creating and edit from there.
